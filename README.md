# overview of planets 
he solar system is made up of eight planets that orbit the Sun. The inner planets are small and rocky, while the outer planets are much larger and mostly made of gas or ice. Each planet is different in size, temperature, and atmosphere. Studying planets helps scientists learn more about space, Earth, and how the universe works.
